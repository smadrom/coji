# Code of conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in coji to be welcoming, safe, and respectful regardless
of background, identity, experience, or level of technical expertise.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and considerate.
- Assume good intent while addressing the actual impact of words and actions.
- Accept feedback, correct mistakes, and keep technical disagreement focused on
  the work.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, trolling, personal
attacks, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations. Enforcement
will be proportional, consistent, and focused on community safety.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact
method on their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/smadrom). Do not include
sensitive conduct reports in a public issue. If a private maintainer channel is
not available or the report concerns the owner, use
[GitHub's abuse-reporting process](https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse).

This policy is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
